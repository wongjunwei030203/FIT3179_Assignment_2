Did You Know?

This map provides a fascinating view of global tertiary education enrolment rates. For instance, Australia stands out with an enrolment rate of 112%, which exceeds 100%. This is because many international students choose Australia as a top destination for higher education, leading to an over-enrolment relative to the domestic population. Australia's high-quality education system, diverse study programmes, and favourable immigration policies make it an attractive option for students from abroad.
